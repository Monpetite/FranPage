About me

Francisco Adrián (Valencia, 1994) psychologist and musician. At his young age he had the opportunity to travel to various countries in America and Europe. Fascinated by art (as he himself expresses it) he visits museums and galleries wherever he goes. He views the great classical and contemporary masters with great enthusiasm and excitement. La Maja desnuda by Francisco de Goya, hits him. The image represents a real woman and not a mythological figure.

His sensitivity to art makes him fruitful and his restless youth leads him to explore in an experimental and personal way what art hides and reveals.

Adrián, in his incipient work, is disciplined, instinctive, on the search for the sensible, the original and unrepeatable; aspects that art requires in order to project oneself as an artist. ...

De la excusa a lo íntimo. Entering into the labyrinth of art when traveling your own way, is also entering a universe of fictions and shadows, avoiding the ideas that become to materialize them.

From his paintings I remember the body as the protagonist, defined by a sinuous, thick and marked line. A random background, without chiaroscuro or nuances, sometimes almost non-existent. I need the excuse to capture a repertoire of feelings and human figures at rest, barely taking off from the stillness.

In his work, it is perceived how the looks intersect between painter and imaginary or real models. There is some intimate communication that leaves the bodies in the creative process, conceiving worlds as significant to them as to the viewer.

Likewise, the background is subordinated to the line, the calligraphy determines the movement, the strength and the volume of the figure, the whites represent emptiness and complement the dynamics of the artist's language.

Entering the plastic story that Adrián offers is simple. The suggestive bodily attitude of his figures, the encountering glances, the extended brushstrokes, the flat colors, the rounded and thick lines evoking sensuality, the force in the line, the horizontality or verticality of the represented object, the directional sense of the work, between others. A whole set of elements that gives order to the space and gives coherence to its artistic approach. Losing yourself in the intimacy of your story is more complicated.

The metaphor of the line in Adrián's work contributes and communicates the complex and subjective aspects of his shadow or construction experience. Recreates and gives new meanings to the represented object, evokes it in its own and different contexts.

The self-portrait is a separate point. As well as being interesting in terms of the meanings it suggests close to the acts of idealization or narcissism. Contemplation of oneself, using a model, is also experiencing the reality or place of the other. By placing itself in the center, as an object that expresses tensions, pleasures, feelings and darkness in images of aesthetic beauty, it gives us part of the enigma that only time will clarify.

Do you know about my Book?

Author: Francisco Adrian, Alejandro Simon
Editorial Académica Española
Languaje: Spanish

Synopsis

Through plastic techniques, little invasive and strongly cathartic, they open the creative process through the construction of artistic pieces; so that, it has more acceptance and flexibility for the personal work of the patients, implying a known dynamic from childhood, such as the use of plastic materials. Patients with iatrogenic allogenosis will be able to find in art therapy a way to carry out their personal process with less resistance, accompaniment processes, a way to express emotions, symbolize by representing the symptoms through the plastic elements to transform them.
